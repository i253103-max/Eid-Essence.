About Us

Eid Essence was founded to bring joy of tradition back to gifting. We specialize in customized gift boxes and premium clothing for your Eid and other festivals. We deal in variety of Men, Women, and Children wear and all kind of gift boxes for your loved ones.

FAQs

How do I customize a Gift Box?

It’s easy! Go to the “Gift Boxes” page, select “Build Your Own”, choose your box color, pick your items (perfumes, chocolates, dates), and add a personalized Eid note at the end.

Will my order arrive before Eid?

We guarantee delivery before Eid morning for all orders placed at least 5 days in advance. For last-minute orders, please check our Express Shipping availability.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes! We deliver to every city in Pakistan with free shipping on orders above PKR 5,000.
